FAC Investment Banking Project

Through this project, I embarked on a deep dive into the intricate workings of investment banking, gaining invaluable insights into the roles of investment bankers and the functioning of investment banking firms. This hands-on experience provided me with practical knowledge and a nuanced understanding of real-world financial challenges. It was a journey of discovery and learning, equipping me with the expertise needed to navigate the complexities of the finance industry with confidence and precision.
